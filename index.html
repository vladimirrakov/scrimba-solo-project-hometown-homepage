Zona Colonial

Located in Santo Domingo, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the oldest continuously inhabited European settlement in the Americas. Stepping into this historic district is like traveling back in time to the 16th century.

Bahia de las Aguilas

Located in the southwestern part of the Dominican Republic, with its crystal-clear turquoise waters, powdery white sand, and stunning rocky cliffs, it is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean.

Merengue

Merengue is a lively and infectious music and dance style that originated in the Dominican Republic. It is considered the national dance and music of the country, deeply rooted in the cultural identity of its people.
